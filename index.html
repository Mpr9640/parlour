The studio

A women-only room, on purpose.

Anjali sits on Road No. 1 in Chandra Nagar, Chinthal — a small, deliberately calm space where the chairs are close enough to talk and far enough to relax. Every stylist, every product shelf, every mirror is arranged for one kind of client: women who want the appointment to be about them, not a production.

We're open all seven days, 10am to 9pm, so a treatment fits around your week instead of the other way round.
